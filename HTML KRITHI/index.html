2.Kamal Hasan

Kamal Haasan is an Indian actor, filmmaker, screenwriter, playback singer, television presenter and politician who works mainly in Tamil cinema and has also appeared in some Telugu, Malayalam, Hindi, Kannada and Bengali films. He is regarded as one of the finest actors in the Indian cinema.Haasan started his career as a child artist in the 1960 Tamil-language film Kalathur Kannamma, for which he won the President's Gold Medal.

3.Vijay

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, popularly known as Thalapathy Vijay, is an Indian actor and playback singer who primarily works in Tamil cinema. Born on June 22, 1974 (age 48) in Chennai, India. Vijay is known for his charismatic screen presence, versatile acting skills, and ability to connect with the audience. Vijay has appeared in numerous successful films and has won several awards for his performances. He has a massive fan following and is regarded as one of the most influential actors in the industry.

5.Ajith


Ajith Kumar is one of the most popular and influential actors in Tamil cinema and has been described by fans and media as “Thala” (Leader) Ajith. He has won six Filmfare Awards South, been nominated for the Filmfare Award for Best Actor – Tamil four times, and is also a recipient of the Kalaimamani award.
